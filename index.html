About CJF Music

CJF Music, a Brooklyn-born producer, grew up immersed in music, influenced by a family legacy where rhythm was a way of life. With a DJ brother and a singing grandfather, music became CJF's passion early on. Inspired by albums like Puff Daddy’s *No Way Out*, The Notorious B.I.G., and Jay-Z’s *Hard Knock Life*, he pursued a career in rap and production. After starting beat-making in 2018, CJF found his unique sound by 2022, launching his journey with *The Blendprint*, a remix of Jay-Z’s *The Blueprint*. Today, CJF’s catalog includes seven albums, each one a tribute to his Brooklyn roots and evolving style.
